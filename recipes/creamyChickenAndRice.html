Creamy Chicken and Rice

In this recipes you are going to learn how to make the best creamy chicken and rice of your life!

Ingredients

  • Rice 60g
  • Chicken Breast 200g
  • Red Bell Pepper 150g
  • Carrot 100g
  • Onion 50g
  • Garlic Powder, Smoke Paprika 1/4 tsp
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Cottage Cheese 100g
  • Gochujang 10g
  • Honey 5g
  • Garlic Powder 1/4 tsp
  • Black Pepper

Instructions

  1. Start by cooking the rice with an optional lemongrass cut into pieces.
  2. Chop up vegetables into smaller pieces. Put chicken breast into a plastic bag and smash it with something heavy like a pot, focussing on the thicker side until it is even. Add garlic powder, smoked paprika, salt and pepper on top of chicken.
  3. Add oil to a pan on medium heat and fry the vegetables for 5-7 minutes. Remove vegetables from the pan, add in another touch of oil, and fry the chicken breast, flipping it every minute until cooked through. Use a food thermometer to be safe, temp should arrive at 74°C or 165°F.
  4. Add to a blender cottage cheese, gochujang, honey, garlic powder, black pepper, and vegetables. Blend for 1 minute until smooth.
  5. Put on a plate rice with the chicken breast and top with the sauce, done!
