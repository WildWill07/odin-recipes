Protein Pancakes

In this recipes you are going to learn how to create delicous banana protein pancakes! The typical yeild of this recipes is 3 medium pancakes.

Ingredients

  • 80g Oat Flower
  • 1 Ripe Banana
  • 1 Scoop of Protein Powder
  • 5g Cinnamon
  • 1/2tsp of Baking Powder
  • 5g of Sweetener
  • 2 Eggs
  • 50g Plain Greek Yogurt

Instructions

  1. Put peeled banana in a bowl and begin to mash with a fork until it reaches smooth consistency.
  2. Add 50g of Plain Greek Yogurt to the bowl with the mashed banana.
  3. Crack two Eggs into the same bowl and mix the wet ingredients together.
  4. Preheat a nonstick pan
  5. While the pan is preheating add in all dry ingredients (80g Oat Flower, 1 Scoop of Proetein Powder, 5g Cinnamon, 1/2tsp of baking powder, and 5g of sweetener) to the bowl of wet ingredients.
  6. Mix all of the ingredients together thouroughly.
  7. Pour desired amount of pancake mix into the pan and cook for roughly 1-2 minutes on each side or until done.
